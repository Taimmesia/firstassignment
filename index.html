My Favourite Things

Rain

I love the petricher that the rain and the soil makes just before it rains. I love the smell of the roses just after the rain and I love the sound of the rain while I am snuggled in bed.

Coffee

I love the smell of coffee when it's being brewed. But my absolute favourite type of coffee is a cappuccino. I really like how pretty it looks and how rich it tastes especially when it is paired with a croissant.

Books

I like books because there's nothing better than a good book.

Summer

And lastly, since I moved to Sweden, I realised how much I took the hot summers in Zambia for granted. I didn't go to the pool or go out on a picnic as much as I should have. But now, in Sweden, some of the best things about the summer are blue skies, strawberries, warm weather, picnics, camping and trips to the beach.
